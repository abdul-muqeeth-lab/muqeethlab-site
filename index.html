01 — About

Abdul Muqeeth

I'm a Health Informatics researcher at Indiana University Indianapolis, working in the Luddy School of Informatics. My work sits at the intersection of biomedical science, large language models, and systems engineering.

I design and build multi-model LLM pipelines that extract, classify, and structure clinical data from unstructured biomedical text. I hold an MS in Bioinformatics from IU Indianapolis and begin my PhD in Health Informatics in Fall 2026.
